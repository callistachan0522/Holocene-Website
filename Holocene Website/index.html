Holocene

Overview

Holocene is a adventure/pvp game. Taken place in the future where the ice age had frozen over the earth and as the years go by and ice starts to slowly melt again animals have mutated and humans have
adapted to the cold. Humans call these new mutated animals "Holo's" players can collect Holo's and use them to fight monsters and level up to fight higher level monsters as
they keep moving through areas of the world.
